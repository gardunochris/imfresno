Users that are retargeted are 70% more likely to convert into sales.  Retargeting ads cost half the price of search ads.  Retargeting can increase conversion rates by as much as 150%.  Retargeting helps address the 98% of visitors who leave a website without making a purchase.

Business Intelligence & Analytics

Customer Experience Optimization 

86% of buyers are willing to pay more for a great customer experience.  88% of consumers are less likely to return to a site after a bad customer experience.  72% of customers will share a positive experience with 6 or more people.

Reputation Management

93% of consumers read local reviews before visiting a business or making a purchase.  91% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations.  57% of consumers will only use a business if it has 4 or more stars.
